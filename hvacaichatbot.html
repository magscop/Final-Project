Project

Overview

I was hired to design an internal AI chatbot for an HVAC company to simplify job management and data entry for their team. The chatbot leverages ChatGPT’s API and integrates with Housecall Pro, allowing staff to log jobs, update customer details, and check schedules through natural conversation.

Combining ChatGPT’s ease of use with Housecall Pro’s structured data, the chatbot eliminated repetitive tasks, improved accuracy, and unlocked operational insights—streamlining workflows across the team.

Problem

Housecall Pro, the HVAC company’s CRM, required a lot of manual work to complete simple everyday tasks. Creating a new job meant going through multiple menus, entering customer details, choosing the service type, assigning a technician, and confirming the schedule. Even small updates—like checking technician availability or editing a record—were repetitive and time-consuming, slowing down the team and increasing the chance of mistakes.

The system’s analytics were also limited. Although it stored plenty of useful data, the team found it hard to identify trends like peak service times, common repairs, or recurring customer issues. Pulling insights manually took too much time and often delayed decisions.

Meanwhile, the team had been experimenting with ChatGPT and liked how natural and easy it was to use. They wanted to bring that same conversational experience to Housecall Pro—using ChatGPT’s language understanding to simplify tasks, cut down on repetitive work, and turn their data into useful insights.

Final Designs &

Solutions

Over 16 weeks, I designed a platform to make the daily work of my HVAC partners simpler and more efficient. I worked closely with them to understand their workflows and pain points, acting as both designer and researcher to ensure the platform solved real user problems. I then managed the project with an overseas development team, guiding implementation, running multiple rounds of testing, and ensuring the chatbot was delivered accurately while meeting both user and business needs.

Chatbot Highlights

Conversational Data Access

The platform allows HVAC partners to interact with the system in a natural, conversational tone. Users can ask the chatbot for real-time job details, customer information, or past notes, and it pulls the relevant data from the Housecall Pro database. This simplifies complex information, reduces the need to navigate multiple screens, and makes it easy for the team to get the answers they need quickly.

Chatbot Highlights

Accessible Conversation Records

A key feature of the platform was a centralized database that allowed HVAC partners to access all past chatbot interactions across different profiles. This ensured that every team member could see previous conversations, reducing duplicate work and improving team coordination. By consolidating communication in one place, the platform made it easier to track job history, follow up with customers, and maintain consistency across daily operations.

Chatbot Highlights

Familiar Interface

The platform was designed with a familiar interface and a chatbot-style interaction, as requested by the HVAC team. This approach made it easy for them to navigate the system, log jobs, and access information, ensuring their team could interact with the platform naturally and efficiently.

Chatbot Highlights

Automated Notifications

The platform sends automated alerts for new tasks, schedule changes, or customer updates. This feature keeps the team informed in real time, reduces missed assignments, and ensures everyone stays aligned without needing constant manual check-ins.
