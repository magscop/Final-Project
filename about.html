Overview

Hi, I’m Maggie, a creative based in Toronto, Ontario. By day, I'm immersed in design across various sectors, and by night, I'm a baking enthusiast on the quest for the ultimate chocolate chip cookie recipe. I'm also a perpetual learner, diving into YouTube tutorials for design tips and tricks. Oh, and "The Office" is my go-to comfort watch—it never gets old!


My Background

I hold a Bachelor's of Design from OCAD University and a UX/UI Design certificate from the University of Toronto. My career has led me through diverse sectors such as tech, energy, finance, healthcare, and non-profits. With each opportunity, I've had the privilege of leveraging my expertise to improve organizations' digital presence and effectiveness.

By aligning my passion for both technology and design, I've discovered a fulfilling path in UX/UI design that keeps me motivated. I thrive on continuously learning new skills and techniques in this dynamic and ever-expanding industry.
