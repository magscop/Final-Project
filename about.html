My expertise spans digital marketing, UX/UI design, B2B & B2C, and all things social media. I'm passionate about fusing design and technology for innovative results. Whether it's crafting email campaigns, building landing pages for campaign implementation, or using my user-centric design skills for content creation, I bring attention to detail and effective decision-making to deliver results.
