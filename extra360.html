Extra360

Extra 360, previously known as Forefront Talent, is an innovative background organization streamlining the path for aspiring background actors venturing into the film industry. I had the privilege of working closely with two dynamic entrepreneurs who led the organization, and was able to successfully revamp their brand and website.

Project Overview

Challenge

The Extra360 website encountered several challenges, all primarily stemming from the absence of a well-defined brand and a confusing website. The lack of a clear brand identity made it difficult to convey a consistent message and establish a recognizable presence in the Canadian background industry. The website, in its original state, not only lacked clarity in communication but also frustrated users, resulting in a significant number of inquiries. Users faced difficulties navigating the website due to its unclear user interface and functionality. This led to a pressing need for a comprehensive redesign to not only improve the user experience but also minimize the dependence on direct inquiries by providing clearer information.

Execution

Branding

To create a strong brand for Extra360, I worked closely with the co-founders to understand their vision and then crafted clear brand guidelines. We emphasized key characteristics such as innovation and professionalism, resulting in a modern and cohesive brand. The development of the logo, colours, and key messaging was a meticulous process, involving multiple iterations to find the right fit that accurately represented the brand characteristics. We tested the brand elements on a group of background actors to see which resonated best and would best contribute to a recognizable brand within the Canadian background industry. In essence, this approach led to the creation of a strong and distinctive brand that aligns seamlessly with the organization's values and goals.

Website Redesign

In the redesign of the Extra360 website, we adhered to the new and clear brand guidelines that showcased innovation and professionalism. To refine our design, we developed mid-fidelity prototypes and sought feedback from background actors who were acquainted with the previous website, "Forefront." The mid-fidelity prototypes served as a valuable tool to collect initial impressions, allowing us to fine-tune the design before progressing to high-fidelity prototypes. By testing the latter with actors familiar with the old website, we gained crucial insights. The positive feedback affirmed that the redesign significantly elevated both the overall design and user experience in comparison to the previous platform. This user-centric approach, integrating brand development with iterative testing, ensured that the revamped Extra360 website not only embodied the brand's vision but also resonated positively with its target audience within the background actor community.

Conclusion

The Results

The Extra360 redesign resulted in a strong brand identity and an intuitive and user-friendly website. By incorporating user research, collaborating with stakeholders, and engaging in iterative design, we successfully crafted a brand and website that makes an impact on the background industry. Through a meticulous process of refinement, we've developed a platform that prioritizes user needs and practical functionality, addressing current challenges while establishing a new standard for accessibility and engagement. Extra360 is now well-positioned to deliver a straightforward and effective experience for aspiring background actors and industry professionals.
