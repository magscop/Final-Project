EXTRA 360

OVERVIEW

001

Project

Overview

Extra 360, previously known as Forefront Talent, is an innovative background organization streamlining the path for aspiring background actors venturing into the film industry.

Problem

Extra360 reached out because their website was confusing and generating too many user inquiries resulting in an overwhelmed customer support team. Users found the onboarding process frustrating and often gave up before fully engaging with the site. Additionally, their unclear brand identity made it challenging to connect effectively with their target audience through marketing efforts.

Recognizing these challenges, I saw an opportunity to redesign their website and improve their marketing strategy.

The mission? Simplify the onboarding process with features that answer common questions and establish a clear brand identity to enhance user experience and improve their marketing effectiveness.

OUTCOME

002

Project

Outcome

The final result is a website that prioritizes user experience and seamlessly guides users through the onboarding process while presenting a clear and consistent brand identity.

Optimized Onboarding

Optimizing the onboarding process was the most crucial need for the Extra360 web redesign. Through initial user interviews and testing, we gained a good understanding of where people were encountering issues. We then designed the initial process and immediately returned to user testing to ensure optimal user experience.

Adding detailed FAQ sections to address common questions, and step-by-step instructions with interactive elements to make users follow the planned process, was crucial to optimizing the onboarding process. Our goal was to ensure that everyone could navigate smoothly and easily find answers to their questions.

As a result of these improvements, we saw a significant reduction in user confusion during the user testing stages. User feedback indicated a much smoother and more intuitive onboarding experience.

Responsive Website

I designed a mobile-first website that ensured a streamlined onboarding process optimized for mobile usability.

Cohesive Brand Identity

The branding overhaul was a significant step in defining Extra360's identity within the production and acting community. With a modern brand identity featuring vibrant graphics, we created a distinctive brand that stands out in marketing efforts and leaves a lasting impression.

A comprehensive design system was also developed for web and digital content, ensuring consistent elements such as the logo, typography, colors, and components across all platforms. This approach maintains a cohesive and recognizable brand, enhancing their impact and user experience.
