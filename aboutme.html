"Design like you're absolutely right, then optimize like you're wrong from the start" - Jared Spool


I thrive on the technical aspects of creating and I am passionate about the process of envisioning an entire system before diving into the details. Crafting a cohesive, well-thought-out experience from the ground up excites me as my design process is rooted in the idea that while you need a strong initial vision, it's crucial to remain flexible and open to evolution. This mindset helps me build impactful systems that adapt and grow with users.

I aim to grow into a thoughtful and approachable design leader, dedicated to guiding and inspiring others. Currently, I’m enjoying the opportunity to dive into creative projects and develop exciting new designs.

Outside of designing and diving into new projects, I love getting lost in a good book, exploring new places, experimenting with paint and illustrations, snapping photos of my surroundings, and sharing my designs on Behance or Dribbble.

Thank you for visiting!
