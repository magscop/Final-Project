"Design like you're absolutely right, then optimize like you're wrong from the start"

I bring brands to life. By combining creative storytelling with research and data-driven insights, I craft clear and authentic voices that give brands a strong, competitive edge in the digital landscape. With a Bachelor’s in Design from OCAD University and specialized training in UX/UI from the University of Toronto, I merge imaginative storytelling with technical expertise to design experiences that truly connect and deliver impact.

What excites me most is building thoughtful, cohesive systems from the ground up. My approach balances having a strong initial vision with staying flexible and open to evolution, ensuring the designs I create aren’t just impactful—they’re adaptable and grow with users.

I aspire to become a design leader who’s approachable, thoughtful, and inspires creativity in others. Right now, I’m fully immersed in exploring creative projects and utilizing new technology like AI to push boundaries.

When I’m not designing, you’ll find me lost in a good book, exploring new places, experimenting with paints and illustrations, capturing moments through photography, or sharing my work on Behance and Dribbble.

Thank you for visiting!
