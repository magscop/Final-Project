HEV "N" LEE HEALING

OVERVIEW

001

Project

Overview

Hev "N" Lee Healing is a thoughtfully designed website that connects individuals seeking holistic healing solutions with Lee, a practitioner offering services such as Reiki, Reflexology, and Feng Shui.

The platform provides a peaceful, welcoming space where clients can learn about the healing practices offered and book sessions with ease, bringing balance and well-being to their lives.

Problem

In today’s fast-paced, high-stress world, many individuals are seeking alternative ways to heal and reconnect with themselves—but often struggle to find trustworthy, accessible, and personalized holistic care. Traditional healthcare systems tend to overlook the importance of energetic and spiritual well-being, leaving people feeling out of balance and unsupported.

Although practices like Reiki, Reflexology, and Feng Shui have gained recognition, the lack of centralized, welcoming platforms makes it difficult for individuals to discover and book reliable holistic services. This disconnect between intention and access creates barriers for those on a healing journey, preventing them from receiving the care they need to restore harmony in their lives.

Conduct research and gather insights to understand user needs, challenges, and opportunities.

User Research Plan

To gain insight into how people discover and engage with holistic wellness services, I conducted five in-depth interviews with individuals interested in alternative healing. These conversations uncovered common pain points with traditional healthcare, key decision factors when choosing a holistic practitioner, and preferences around booking and digital engagement. After developing the website, I shared the final version with participants and conducted a follow-up survey to assess the platform’s overall effectiveness, usability, and clarity in communicating the services offered.

80%

Percentage of participants said they found it difficult to differentiate between holistic services online.

Many felt overwhelmed by vague or overly spiritual language. One participant noted, "I just want to understand what Reiki is and whether it’s right for me—without having to decode it."

60%

Reported they wanted more transparency and professionalism in holistic websites.

Respondents said they were more likely to book if the site clearly explained the practitioner’s background, what to expect in a session, and pricing. One user mentioned, "If I can’t find answers easily, I’m not going to book.

90%

Said they prefer a peaceful, calming online experience that reflects the healing journey.

Participants emphasized the importance of intuitive design that made them feel at ease. As one respondent shared, "The energy of the site tells me a lot about the person behind it.
